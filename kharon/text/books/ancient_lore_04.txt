
Gentle Giant
Ancient Chronicler
STRONGHOLD,DUNGEON

An elysian giant appeared before us tonight. I say it was night because my companions were asleep until I roused them; the sky is nowhere in sight. The giant spoke into my soul, into all our souls, for it had no mouth we could see...
-->>
"Why do you seek Elysium?"^We sought peace, respite from our harsh lives. We told the giant, and it held its arm to the center of the cavern, where a blue glow began to appear.
-->>
"Leave your items here."^With trepidation, we left our remaining torches, and the crude weapons we had brought for defense, on the floor.^The glow grew large
-->>
enough for a man to walk through, and so my companions entered. As I end this passage, I shall join them.
