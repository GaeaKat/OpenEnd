MISSING GATE

By a miracle, we found our way to the cavern of the gate. Carved into the stone walls were messages that guaranteed us that this is, surely, the place. Yet the gate is gone!

And so too is our food... we may starve here. I have proposed we wait a day, and then turn back. If we become desparate, we... No, I will not entertain that thought. The gate is here, and I will die waiting to go through it before I turn back.
