ENDER GIFTS

Through long research, we have concluded that our gifts, the gifts of the Enders, are the gifts of awareness and telepathy. We have the power to speak to the souls of other beings, and are aware of souls looking at us.

I am the first Ender, and I am drawn to the heart of Elysium. I shall enter the mines of my outsider forebears and discover the soul that is calling to me.
