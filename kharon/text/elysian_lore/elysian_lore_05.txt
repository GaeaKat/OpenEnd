OFFSPRING

The children grew up together and their souls were close. We were all surprised when they told us what they wanted. Of course, there were a few objections. The giants saw the union as one of souls, and if their souls were close enough, their bodies meant nothing. However, the mystics warned that there could be no children and it was not a proper union.

It still went ahead, for my choice was the last that mattered. I allowed it.

And we were all shocked when their child came to be. It looks like our young, but it is pale and shows no interest in food or drink when presented with them. The wisest of our mystics and the giants are all perplexed, but are now curious to see what will become of my grandchild...
